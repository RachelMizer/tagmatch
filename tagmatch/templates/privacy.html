Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register and use TagMatch, we collect:

  • Account information: username, email address, first and last name, password (hashed)
  • Profile information: location, date of birth, gender identity, sexuality, height, build, relationship types seeking, children status, profile photo
  • Tags: interests and preferences you select
  • Messages: content of messages sent between users on the platform
  • Usage data: standard server logs (pages visited, timestamps)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Power the tag-based matching system
  • Enable messaging between users
  • Display your profile to other users
  • Send account-related emails (verification, password reset)
  • Review reports submitted by users for safety purposes

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. What Other Users Can See

Other logged-in users can view your profile, including your username, profile photo, location, and the personal details you have provided. Your email address is never displayed to other users. Your messages are private between you and the recipient.

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your profile, messages, and associated data are permanently removed from our systems.

5. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. Passwords are stored as hashed values and are never readable by our team. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Cookies and Sessions

TagMatch uses session cookies to keep you logged in. These are essential for the site to function and are not used for advertising tracking.

7. Your Rights

You may update or delete your profile information at any time from the Edit Profile page. You may permanently delete your account from the same page. To request a copy of the data we hold about you, contact us via the admin page.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will note the date of the last update at the top of this page.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us through the site's admin page.
